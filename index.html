INTRODUCTION

Lumi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights when you use the Lumi app for creating stories and collages.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Photos and Media: Lumi accesses photos from your device's library solely to create stories and collages. This content is processed locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Purchase and Subscription Data: If you subscribe to Lumi Premium, Apple processes your payment. We do not store your payment card details. We receive limited transaction data (e.g. subscription status, purchase date) through Apple and Adapty to manage your access to premium features.

Analytics and Usage Data: We collect anonymized data about how you use the app, including feature interactions, session duration, and user flows. This data is processed via PostHog and Firebase and does not include personally identifiable information.

Crash and Diagnostic Data: We collect crash reports and technical diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix bugs. This includes device model, OS version, and app version.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Adapty — subscription and paywall management

  • PostHog — product analytics

We do not sell your data to third parties.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and unlock premium features based on your subscription status
  • To improve app stability, performance, and UX
  • To understand feature usage and prioritize development
  • To process and restore your subscription

DATA RETENTION

Analytics and crash data is retained in accordance with the policies of the respective third-party services. Subscription data is retained as long as necessary to manage your account.
